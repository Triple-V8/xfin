How does it work?

To stake your USDC, simply create a BSC wallet and deposit USDC into your wallet on the BSC chain
Once your deposit has been processed, you come to our website homepage, enter an amount you would like to stake and click stake
Claim rewards At the end of every calm down period which is usually a week, you can claim 8% of your staking as rewards. Ensure to claim your rewards every 7 days to restart the calm down period
Unstake Capital At the end of the 90 days duration, your stakes would have reach maturity and you can choose to unstake your capital
